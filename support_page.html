Frequently Asked Questions

How do I play Chain Rush?

Swipe to connect numbers in sequence (e.g., 3→4→5). The longer your chain, the higher your score! Complete daily challenges and unlock achievements as you progress.

What are power-ups?

Power-ups help you create better chains and achieve higher scores. You can earn them through gameplay or purchase them in the shop.

How do daily challenges work?

A new challenge appears every day at midnight. Complete it before it expires to earn special rewards and climb the leaderboard!

I found a bug, how do I report it?

Please email us at parmeetdx@gmail.com with details about the issue, including your device model and iOS version.

How do I restore my purchases?

Your purchases are linked to your Apple ID. Go to Settings → Shop → Restore Purchases to restore any previous purchases.
